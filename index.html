PROTECT YOUR DATABASES

These repositories - which often contain the lifeblood of your business - must be protected to prevent improper access and leakage or disclosure of the sensitive data contained within them. With SECOPA, you can discover, assess and report on vulnerabilities, misconfigurations and improper access controls within databases and big data stores and map to industry security and compliance requirements.

WE HAVE A WIDE BREADTH OF EXPERIENCE WITH THREAT DETECTION

As threats become more complex and advanced, real-time threat detection (and notification) becomes increasingly important. Advanced big data correlation of SECOPA threat intelligence, plus expert analysis in our Advanced Security Operations Centers (ASOCs), allows SECOPA to block new and emerging threats sooner and with more accuracy.
