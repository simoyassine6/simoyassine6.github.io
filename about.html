QUALITY MANAGEMENT

PASSION

At Coalfire, we employ a process-driven quality management system (QMS) that ensures effective and repeatable project, staffing, and contract management activities based on Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI), Project Management Body of Knowledge, and ISO standards, as well as best practices. Through an emphasis on efficiency and quality, we strive to improve your experience, refine project metrics, and deliver unparalleled results.

COMMITMENT

By choosing a cybersecurity partner that weaves this approach into operational and strategic direction, you will work with experts who adhere to their own principles. You’ll also interact with a dedicated quality assurance team from service delivery through reviews, assessments, and audits. Our auditors are used to being audited themselves, so they “walk the talk.” Using quality control activities performed by trained quality analysts or technical peers in the industry, our product development team improves service delivery plans that directly influence the training programs and support for Coalfire consultants. Put simply, Coalfire invests in its team.

PARTNERSHIP
