COMPANY OVERVIEW

SECOPA is the cybersecurity advisor that helps private and public sector organizations avert threats, close gaps, and effectively manage risk. By providing independent, tailored advice and services that span the cybersecurity lifecycle (Cyber Risk Services, Compliance Services) we help clients develop scalable programs that improve their security posture, achieve their business objectives, and fuel their continued success.

At SECOPA, we employ a process-driven quality management system (QMS) that ensures effective and repeatable project, staffing, and contract management activities based on Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI), Project Management Body of Knowledge, and ISO standards, as well as best practices. Through an emphasis on efficiency and quality, we strive to improve your experience, refine project metrics, and deliver unparalleled results.

By choosing a cybersecurity partner that weaves this approach into operational and strategic direction, you will work with experts who adhere to their own principles. You’ll also interact with a dedicated quality assurance team from service delivery through reviews, assessments, and audits. Our auditors are used to being audited themselves, so they “walk the talk.” Using quality control activities performed by trained quality analysts or technical peers in the industry, our product development team improves service delivery plans that directly influence the training programs and support for SECOPA consultants. Put simply, SECOPA invests in its team.
