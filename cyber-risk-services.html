CYBER RISK SERVICES

MTrusted Cybersecurity Advisors Since 2010

The board of directors, executive management, and security leaders recognize that cyber risk threatens enterprise value. Thus, most organizations are now bolstering their defenses and taking steps to improve enterprise resiliency. SECOPA has been advising clients on cyber risk since the company was founded in 2010, and we now serve as trusted cybersecurity advisors to thousands of enterprises. We know the risks facing almost every industry, and we’ve provided the leadership, advice, staffing support, and technical skills that security leaders have grown to trust.

Experienced Leaders

Each of SECOPA’s Cyber Risk Service projects is led by a credentialed and experienced senior executive, and each project is customized to address your unique situation. These leaders and the well-trained consultants assigned to your project will:

  • Identify source of risk and quantify potential impacts associated with cyber-related business disruptions, compromised data and intellectual property, legal exposure and reputational damage.
  • Develop security strategy, policies and governance programs, and propose a prioritized remediation plan based on industry best practices.
  • Implement controls that help organizations identify, detect, prevent, respond, and recover from cyber threats.

Customized Services

SECOPA offers customized Assessment, Advisory and Assurance services. These services help you get a handle on your current cyber risk posture, implement the security improvements you seek to make, and provide an assurance to the board and senior management that risk is being managed according to policy.
